  • Cleaned missing values to ensure data integrity

  • IQR: $4173.19 ‚Üí Confirms pricing variability

üîπ Statistical Testing

  • Hypothesis: H‚ÇÄ: Mean PSM = $6800 vs H‚ÇÅ: Mean PSM > $6800
